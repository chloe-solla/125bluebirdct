Silly Little Project

Cool Super Cool Link

About two winters ago I thought it’d be a great idea to learn how to crochet, especially when we were stuck in quarantine. When experimenting with different needle sizes, techniques, yarn sizes, materials, etc. and a lot of trial and error I was able to make crocheting a little side hustle. I ended up making about $500 over the spring, summer, and fall of 2021 and it was fun learning how to market and edit my own photos. I was able to sell bags and clothing for a decent price but as soon as school started I lacked free time. Every now and then I crochet bags and sell them whenever I have time consuming events like wrestling tournaments or road trips.
